Santiago Torres-Borda

About

Currently working on my Ph.D. thesis about time sensitive networks and their performance at ISAE SUPAERO in Toulouse, France. I focus mainly on the performance evaluation of shapers defined in IEEE 802.1Q otherwise known as Time Sensitive Networking or TSN. I use tools such as Network Calculus to perform my work. My main interests are asynchronous mechanisms, compositional approaches and optimization for the use of the latter. All of this is done in the context of aerospatial systems such as airplanes, satellites, etc.

Research

Some non exhaustive key words that describe well my topics of interest are : CBS, ATS, TSN, network design, network optimization, network configuration, embedded systems.
